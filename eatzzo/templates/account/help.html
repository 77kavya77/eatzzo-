Help Center

Content Sharing

  • How do I upload food videos?

    To share your food videos, go to the Create section in the app or website. Click "Upload Video" and select your file. Add a title, description, and relevant tags. Videos should be under 10 minutes and in MP4 format for best results.

  • What are the guidelines for food articles?

    Articles should be original content about food, recipes, or culinary experiences. They must be at least 300 words and can include photos. We encourage detailed recipes, restaurant reviews, or food culture pieces. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.

  • How do I edit or delete my shared content?

    Go to your profile and select the content you want to modify. Click the three-dot menu to find edit or delete options. Note that if content has significant engagement, deletion may require moderator approval.

Home Food Sales

  • How do I start selling home-cooked food?

    First, complete your chef profile verification. Then go to "My Kitchen" in your account and click "Add Listing." You'll need to provide food details, pricing, available quantity, pickup/delivery options, and food safety certifications if required in your area.

  • What are the food safety requirements?

    Requirements vary by location. Generally, you'll need to maintain proper food handling practices, provide ingredient lists, and disclose allergens. Some regions require food handler certifications. We'll guide you through local requirements during setup.

  • How do I manage orders and payments?

    The "My Orders" dashboard shows all incoming orders. You can accept, decline, or message customers there. Payments are processed securely through EatZzo, with weekly deposits to your bank account minus a 15% service fee.

Getting Started

  • How do I create an account?

    To create an EatZzo account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You can register using your email address or through your Google or Facebook account. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your profile.

  • How do I set up my chef profile?

    After creating your account, go to your profile settings and select "Chef Profile Setup." You'll need to provide information about your culinary expertise, upload photos of your dishes, and set your availability for taking orders.

  • How do I browse content and food listings?

    Use the search bar at the top of any page to find content by keywords. You can browse categories on the Explore page or follow creators to see their latest posts in your feed. Filter options are available for both content and food listings.
