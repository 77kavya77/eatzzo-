Remember Real Food?

Eatzzo was born from a simple realization: in our fast-paced world, many of
us are missing the comfort, health benefits, and cultural connection that
comes with homely cooked meals. While fast food and restaurants are
convenient, they often lack the nutritional value and emotional satisfaction of
food made with care in home kitchens.

As two friends passionate about technology and food, we saw an opportunity
to bridge this gap. We envisioned a platform that world:

  • Connect people craving homely food with talented home cooks in their
    community

  • Preserve and share cultural food traditions

  • Provide healthy alternatives to fast food

  • Create earning opportunities for home-based cooks

  • Reduced food waste through donations to those in need

More Than Just Food Delivery

Home Cooks Platform

We empower home cooks with licenses to turn their passion
into income. Through our platform, they can share their
culinary creations through videos, sell their homemade
meals, and connect with food lovers in their area.

Community Engagement

Through food videos, recipes, and interactive features,
we're creating a space where food lovers can learn, share,
and celebrate homemade cuisine together.

Homely Food Products

Beyond prepared meals, we provide a marketplace for
homemade food products - from special curry powders to
traditional pickles and preserves. This includes both
individual home producers and small businesses.

Food Donation Network

Our donation feature allows users to contribute
meals to those in need, supporting old age homes and
orphanages with nutritious, homemade food.
